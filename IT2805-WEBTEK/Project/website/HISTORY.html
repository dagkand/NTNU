History of espionage

Espionage is referred to as the act of gaining confidential information. Formerly, it was executed unorganized, however, it got professionalized in the early 20-th century. Espionage is often associated with governments spying on enemies for military purposes, whereas espionage which involves corporations is referred to as industrial espionage.

History of STONE

STONE was founded in Norway in 1916, with the purpose of increasing the safety of Norway. However, the commercial possibilities associated with such an organization quickly caught the attention of STONE’s CEO, which in turn transformed STONE into a for-profit. In the following years, STONE performed numerous missions, swiftly increasing STONE’s reputation. For the sake of global safety, details of STONE’s missions cannot be published.
